Good to know
Questions, answered
How long will my order take?
Every gift is made to order. Production takes 2–3 business days, plus courier delivery of 1–3 business days depending on your area. Ordering for a specific date? Mention it in your notes and we'll confirm timing on WhatsApp.
What does delivery cost?
How does personalisation work?
Add your names, dates, or message in the notes at checkout. We confirm the personalisation details with you on WhatsApp before anything is printed — so you know exactly what you're getting.
How do I pay?
No card details on the site. Your order is sent to us on WhatsApp, we confirm it and send an invoice payable by EFT or SnapScan. Production starts once payment reflects.
